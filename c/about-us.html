Our Story

LEDGERS is built and managed by a team of passionate individuals having diverse educational backgrounds. Our team has rich experience in building large scalable platforms that support millions of small businesses. Our mission is to better organise finances of individuals and small businesses to help them compete better and grow into large businesses.

Where are you based out of?

Our engineering team is based out of India and we have offices in various countries to provide sales and service support.

Where is the data stored?

LEDGERS is built on the AWS cloud with some features powered by Google and Azure cloud. Your data will be stored in multiple data-centres. We regularly backup all data and ensure we have fail-overs built-in to ensure data safety and security.

Will my data be safe?

We will never sell or share your information to any third-parties. Certain versions of LEDGERS product is free of cost for unlimited users. The data you store or create on LEDGERS can be exported out of LEDGERS seamlessly in excel format.

How do I get support?

We have a team of over 100+ experts who can help you with LEDGERS and a wide-variety of GST queries. Just call, email or chat with us to get support.

Have Question ? Get in touch!

Need help with LEDGERS? Our dedicated support team is always available to help you setup and use LEDGERS.
