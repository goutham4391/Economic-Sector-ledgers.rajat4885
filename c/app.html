Start billing from your smartphone

LEDGERS is a powerful billing and collection app that can help you easily send invoices with payment links to collect digital payments.

  • Send quotes and invoices to phone contacts in seconds.
  • Collect payment through UPI, credit or debit card and net banking.
  • Keep track of incoming online payments and issue receipts easily.

Powerful POS with integrated barcode scanner

LEDGERS mobile app has an integrated barcode scanner capable of accurately and quickly billing products. The barcode scanner is backed by a completely online inventory management system that can help you easily track low stock products and inventory levels by branches.
